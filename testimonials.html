Shireen Gopaldas

ACCESS is the only premier corporate sustainability case competition in the country. You encounter case competitions on marketing, finance, and operations on a regular basis, but you never see one singularly focused on sustainability. The opportunities for learning that this competition provided were wholly unique from others', which strongly encouraged me to join. Being a business major, I really wanted to learn how to creatively integrate sustainability into the foundation of a company. I thought that it was an entirely necessary advocacy given the current state of the Philippines and the world as a whole. ACCESS definitely fulfilled my expectations in every way. I think that I now hold the advocacy close to my heart, especially after having learned how important and beneficial it can be to incorporate sustainability into the core of a business. As I enter the corporate world, I will undoubtedly carry the sustainability practices that I learned from the competition with me wherever I go, in the hopes that I will be able to improve how business is conducted in the Philippines.

And no matter the outcome, in case competitions, it’s really more about the journey (+ learnings!!!) than the destination.
